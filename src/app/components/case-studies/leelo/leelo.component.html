LEELO

User experience - Problem Solving

Organize your readings and share it with your friends.


A web platform to enhance and to boost reading habits in college students.


Role

Product Designer



Timeframe

March - April 2018

Non english native students spend considerable time reading and understanding english articles and documents. And they usually tend to not understand the author ideas or not finishing the readings.


This college project aim to boost an habit through Human Computer Interaction. We studied the college students reading habit issues, and proposed a solution through HCI (Human Computer Interaction) which its viability and interest was fast-validated.

This case study was made with:


Specific users

Design students from the Icesi University, considering the quantity of english readings they have to do during their program.


A group of 10 students did a read & comprehension test in english, in order to know the reading & comprehension level. We found that the level is at an average of 63%, meaning there is something interfering the reading habit.


Cualitative methods

To find those interferences, we did interviews, observation (shadowing) and finally a survey in order to know the users interests related with reading habits

A short interview to know how the users pereceived the solution


Cuantitative method

To evaluate the user engagement, the users rated, through a UES short form , these 4 aspects of the solution: Focus attention (FA), Perceived usability (PU), Aesthetic appeal (AE) and Endurability (EN).

Thanks to a state of art, interviews and reading comprehension tests with different users, considering variables as: focus, interest and comprehension, we could understand the actual experience of the students in this case.

We found that the core pain issues are:


  • Search vocabulary translation and meaning.

  • Highlight and comment inside the articles.

  • Organize reading summaries.

  • Presentation of the reading itself.

PROJECT GUIDELINES

We made an style guide to keep consistent the design of the interfaces across the web platform.

Guided by this userflow, as a team we discussed and made a few wireframes and then we assigned different branches (of the userflow) to each one of the team to start prototyping the product. Userflows also let us have a clear view about the funcionalities needed by the user and how are they connected.

LEELO WEB APP

According to the insights found in the field work, we propose these functions in our proposal:

Translate unknown words

To search vocabulary translation and meaning in less time and distractions.

Highlight content and take notes

To mark the most important content about the readings with personal annotations.

Keep all actions in the same workplace

All highlights, notes, summaries are stored and can be accessed in the reading section by tabs.

VALIDATIONS

The user testers perceived that Leélo will help them to be more organized, plan and improve their time with reading activities.

They also comment that is a good looking app but they can not understand a few aspects about the interaction with them. That is revealed in the UES evaluation of the experience.




Where the focus attention aspect can be improved by making clear how the web-app works, by doing so it can considerably increase the reward and usability perception of using Leélo.

This was a very nice project where we learned a lot about talking with people and finding what they need to have a better experience with their everyday activities like reading.
